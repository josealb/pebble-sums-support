Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Overview

Pebble Sums is a simple arithmetic learning application. The app is designed to work without collecting personal data.

Data Collection

Pebble Sums does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information. The app does not require an account and does not send app data to any external server.

Analytics and Tracking

Pebble Sums does not use analytics, tracking, advertising services, or third-party SDKs that collect user data.

Local Settings

The app may save simple settings on your device, such as the selected voice language. These settings stay on your device and can be removed by deleting the app.

Children

Pebble Sums is designed for children and families. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the App Store listing.
